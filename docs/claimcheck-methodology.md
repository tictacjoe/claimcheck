Claim Check tracks documented government actions across four databases.

**The four databases**

1. **Corporate Deregulation** — actions reducing or removing regulatory oversight of corporate/industry activity. Draws most heavily of the three curated trackers on direct government sources — agency filings, official statements — supplemented by investigative and mainstream reporting.
2. **Cabinet-Level Legal Exposure** — documented prosecution exposure for current officials, framed to legal standards. Prioritizes primary sources — court dockets, official statements — where they exist, relying on investigative and mainstream reporting where they don't.
3. **Government Service Redirection** — changes in how government services are delivered, funded, or reassigned. Leans most on investigative and mainstream reporting of the three curated trackers, with primary government sources used where available.
4. **Reporting** — a running archive of relevant news coverage, distinct in scale and purpose from the three curated trackers above. Sourced almost entirely to established news organizations rather than primary documents, by design.
